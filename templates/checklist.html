STARTING ENGINE

JFS START 2 - CHECK JFS light ON
THROTTLE Advance to IDLE at 20% RPM
Idle Detent Toggle (unless idle/cutoff code is enabled)
SEC caution light Check OFF around 20%
ENGINE warning light OFF at 60% RPM
JFS switch Confirm OFF (snaps OFF at 55%)
HYD/OIL PRESS light OFF between 15 and 70%

ENGINE IDLE CHECKS (OPTIONAL)

FUEL FLOW 700 - 1700 PPH
OIL pressure MIN 15 PSI
NOZ POS Greater than 94%
RPM 62% - 80%
FTIT Below 650°C
HYD PRESS A and B 2850 - 3250psi - around 12 o'clock position
Throttle cutoff release Check - Attempt to retard the throttle to OFF without depressing the cutoff release.
